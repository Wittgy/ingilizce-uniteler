⚡ Modal Verbs — should / must / mustn't / needn't

Use modal verbs to talk about rules, advice, and what is necessary.

ModalMeaningUse
mustçok önemli / zorunluVery important rule
mustn'tkesinlikle yasakAbsolutely forbidden
should-meli / öneriGood advice / suggestion
needn'tgerek yokNot necessary
Examples
We must protect nature. (Very important!)
We mustn't pollute the river. (Forbidden!)
We should recycle paper and plastic. (Good idea)
You needn't worry — the animals are safe. (Not necessary)
⭐ Remember: modal + base verb  →  We should protect (NOT: should protects / should protecting)
⏳ Past Continuous Tense

Use Past Continuous for actions happening at a moment in the past.

SubjectHelperVerb form
I / He / She / Itwasverb + -ing
You / We / Theywereverb + -ing
Examples
The children were hiking in the forest yesterday.
A frog was jumping near the lake at that moment.
While the birds were flying, the children were watching.
When it started to rain, they were camping.
⏰ Time words: yesterday · last night · at that moment · while · when
