The Passive Voice

Edilgen Çatı — is/are + V3

💡
What is Passive Voice?

Sometimes who did the action is not important — what matters is what was done. In these cases, we use the Passive Voice.

Active (Etken)
Subject does the action
Teachers organise competitions.
Öğretmenler yarışmaları düzenler.
Passive (Edilgen)
Object becomes subject
Competitions are organised (by teachers).
Yarışmalar düzenlenir.
is / are + V3 (3rd form of verb)
Always use V3 (third form) in Passive Voice. Use is for singular subjects, are for plural subjects.
📊
is vs are — Singular vs Plural
ActivePassive
Active Teachers clean the classroom.Passive The classroom is cleaned.
Active Students do experiments.Passive Experiments are done.
Active Teachers organise competitions.Passive Competitions are organised.
Active The Art Club shapes ideas.Passive Ideas are shaped.
Active Students sing songs.Passive Songs are sung.
Active They teach Maths.Passive Maths is taught.
✨
More Passive Examples
Maths is taught. Problems are solved. Words are learned. Stories are read. Songs are sung. Games are played. The classroom is cleaned every morning. The swimming pool is used on Fridays. Sports events are organised by teachers. A new computer lab is being built. Responsibilities are shared. Projects are worked on.
Mini Reminder: Passive Voice = ✔ is / are + ✔ V3 (third form) + ✔ the doer is not important

Theme 9 verbs with Turkish meanings and passive examples

Reading: A Busy Day at School

Reading comprehension — Theme 9 School Life & Education

Hello! My name is Kerem, and I am a student at an international school. Our school has many extracurricular activities and great facilities. There is a Science Club, a Chess Club, and a Drama Club. I am in the Science Club because I like doing experiments. New topics are researched every week, and projects are prepared by students. The classrooms are cleaned every morning, and the swimming pool is used on Fridays. Sports events are organised by the teachers. At the moment, some students are practising for the Spelling Bee competition. This term, we have joined different competitions, and we have been studying very hard for the exams. A new computer lab is being built at our school. I love my school because responsibilities are shared, and everyone works together.
